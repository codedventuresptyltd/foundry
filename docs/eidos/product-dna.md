# Product DNA

:::info Coming Soon
Product DNA modeling documentation coming soon.
:::

Eidos models products as having "genetic code" that defines their characteristics, behaviors, and rules.

## Components of Product DNA

### Attributes

The characteristics that define a product.

### Constraints

Rules about valid configurations.

### Behaviors

How the product responds to context.

### Relationships

Connections to other products or systems.
