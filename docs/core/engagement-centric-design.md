# Engagement-Centric Design

:::info Coming Soon
Engagement pattern documentation is being prepared.
:::

We model commerce as **engagements**, not just orders.

## Why Engagements?

Traditional systems focus on orders as the primary entity. We believe commerce is a **conversation**, not just a transaction.

## Benefits

- Full lifecycle visibility
- Context preservation
- Multiple orders per engagement
- Rich history and audit trails
- Better customer experience

## Beyond Commerce

The engagement pattern applies to any multi-step, stateful process.
