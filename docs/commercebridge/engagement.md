Engagements represent the full lifecycle of a commerce interaction.

## Why Engagements?

Traditional systems model **orders** as the primary entity. CommerceBridge models **engagements**:

- Commerce is a conversation, not just a transaction
- State persists across the entire journey
- Multiple orders can exist within one engagement
- Context and history are first-class citizens

## Engagement Lifecycle

1. Inquiry and quoting
2. Cart building and pricing
3. Order placement
4. Fulfillment tracking
5. Post-order modifications
